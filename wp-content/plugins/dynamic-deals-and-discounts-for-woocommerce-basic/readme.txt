Dynamic Deals and discounts on festival seasons for your WooCommerce Store.

== Description ==

Increase your store orders by providing deals and discounts on time based.

**Deals based on**
Deals will be applied on Cart.

* Based on Products (Both Simple and Variable)
* Based on Categories

**Discounts Based on**
Discounts will be applied on Cart.

* Discount applied by Flat Price
* Discount applied by Percentage

**Customization**
Add rich, beautifully-presented media -- no graphic design expertise necessary:

* Show Badge - Enabling this option will show offer badge on product image.
* Show Discount Text - Enabling this option will show discount text on product.
* Replace Sales Badge - Enabling this option will replace sales badge of products with offer badge.

**Upcoming Deals Ad**

* Show Ad Image - Enable this option will show advertisement image.
* Ad Image Position - Select the position of Image that needs to be displayed.
* Ad Image Duration - Select the Ad Image duration.
* Show Ad Image in Checkout - Enable this option to show ad image on checkout page.

**Pro Features**

 - **Timer**: Deal Timer for showing the ending time of the deal on Shop, Product Category, Single Product, Custom Offer pages.
 - **Customization**: Custom Badge Styles and Deal Timer styles can be easily customized by providing the custom CSS. A tool for generating the Badge is there!
 - **Filter**: Orders filter based on deals purchase. This help you to identify the orders on particular deal and to get report.
 - **Shortcode**: Shortcode for displaying the Deal products on a separate page. This helps you to attract the customer on your store.
 - **Ad Sliders**: Providing the Upcoming Deals AD on your store by the Slider. You can have Slider on Shop, Cart and Single Product Page.
 - **User Role**: Deals Based on User Role will allow your customer to take discounts on their user roles. Avoid other plugins for Manipulating User Role Discounts.
 - **Support & Updates**: If any features added you will get the automatic update. And also you will have no Queue on your Support Requests.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3. Thats it! you can now configure the plugin.
